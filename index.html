About Me

Look below to learn about my hobbies, or click the "Images" tab to see pictures!

Reading

Reading is one of my favorite passtimes. I read on topics that i find interesting, but I also read things that help me gain insight on myself and the world. Some of my favorite books are on topics like art history, psychology, and homeopathy.

Music

Music is a very important passion of mine. Since I was old enough to know what I do and don't like, I have has a taste for all kinds of music as well as an ear for sound. I have always been able to listen to a peice and pick out the notes on any kind of instrument, which I think is pretty cool. My favorite genre of music is electronic, but I have more specific favorite subgenres like experimental and dark ambient. Click on the button below to view a smaple playlist of my favorites.

Gaming

While I try not to spend so much time on this, gaming is also a big interest of mine. Some of my favorite video games are the Elder Scrolls, Minecraft, Apex Legends, and Silent Hill.

Learning

Both in school and in my freetime, I like to make it a point to invest some time into learning about the things that either interest me or that i can benefit from. I enjoy learning about the natural world, psychology, religion, art and art history, and much more.

Art

Art is the hobby that I invest the most time and energy into. I work with a wide range of mediums and styles, and I also dedicate much time to research and learning the technicalities of art so that I can improve. I look to the artworks of many of my favorite artists for inspiration and reference as well. Most of my favorite artists are impressionists, but I do follow a few abstract artists such as Oliver DeSagazan and Jiri Barta.

School

Although it sometimes wears me down, school is also one of my hobbies. I use school primarily as a way to push myself and test my limits and abilities. It is through my performance in school that I can judge my growth in certain areas, and because I apply myself as much as I can the results after each year are very satisfying.
